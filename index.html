Welcome to Mönsterås

A gem on the Swedish southeast coast.

The top 4 on the To Do-list around Mönsterås

Nature

There are many beautiful areas to explore around Mönsterås, both forests and around the seacoast.

Activities

There is an inside-waterpark for rainy days. Sunny days you can go canoeing, ride horses, go skate in the skatepark or playground fun.

Holiday

Maybe you are here for a summer holiday. We have beautiful beaches all around and camping or you rent av cabain.

Guide

There are several locations you can see and explore here, for example a monastery ruin.
